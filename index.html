FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about homoeopathic treatment.

Is homoeopathy scientifically proven?
Yes, homoeopathy is a recognised system of medicine with over 200 years of clinical evidence. The Central Council of Homoeopathy in India regulates its practice. Numerous peer-reviewed studies support its efficacy for chronic conditions, allergies, and paediatric health.
How long does homoeopathic treatment take?
Duration depends on the nature and chronicity of the condition. Acute conditions like colds or fever respond within days. Chronic conditions like eczema or thyroid disorders typically improve over 3–6 months with regular treatment.
Are homoeopathic medicines safe for children?
Absolutely. Homoeopathic medicines are extremely gentle, non-toxic, and highly safe for infants, children and pregnant women. They have no side effects and are often the preferred choice for paediatric care.
Can I take homoeopathic medicine alongside allopathic medicines?
Yes, homoeopathic medicines generally do not interfere with allopathic medications. Dr. Anjali will review your current medications and advise accordingly. Many patients gradually reduce allopathic dependency as homoeopathic treatment progresses.
What should I bring to my first consultation?
Please bring any previous medical reports, test results, and a list of current medications. A detailed case history is taken at the first consultation, including physical, mental and emotional aspects of your health.
Do you offer online or video consultations?
Currently, Dr. Anjali primarily sees patients at the clinic for the best case-taking experience. However, WhatsApp and phone consultations are available for follow-ups and patients who cannot visit in person. Contact us for details.
