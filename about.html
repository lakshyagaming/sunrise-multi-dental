Introduction

Dr. Anjali is a highly experienced and deeply committed homoeopathic physician practising at Eldeco Arcadia, Greater Noida. With over a decade of clinical experience, she has developed a reputation as one of the most trusted natural healthcare providers in the region.

Her practice is rooted in the classical homoeopathic tradition — a system of medicine founded by Dr. Samuel Hahnemann over 200 years ago — which treats the individual as a whole, addressing not just the physical symptoms but also the mental and emotional dimensions of health.

Dr. Anjali is known for her meticulous case-taking, her deep understanding of chronic constitutional conditions, and her empathetic, patient-first approach that has earned her glowing reviews from thousands of satisfied patients.

Experience

With over 10 years of active clinical practice, Dr. Anjali has treated a wide spectrum of acute and chronic conditions. Her patient base spans all age groups — from newborns and children to the elderly — and includes cases of varying complexity.

She has successfully treated thousands of patients suffering from skin diseases, hair fall, allergies, respiratory issues, hormonal imbalances, gastrointestinal disorders, neurological conditions, and musculoskeletal problems — many of whom had failed to find relief through conventional medicine.

Her experience in constitutional prescribing, miasmatic analysis, and classical repertorisation allows her to approach even the most complex and chronic cases with confidence and clarity.

Philosophy of Homoeopathy

Dr. Anjali firmly believes in Hahnemann's golden principle: "Similia similibus curantur" — like cures like. She practices classical homoeopathy, which focuses on understanding the patient's complete symptom picture — physical, mental, and emotional — and prescribing the single most similar remedy that matches this totality.

Patient Care Approach

Every consultation with Dr. Anjali begins with a thorough, unhurried case-taking that may last 45–60 minutes for a new patient. She listens carefully to the patient's story — the nature of symptoms, the triggers, the modalities, the emotional state, the life history — to form a complete and unique picture of each individual.

Treatment is then tailor-made for that individual. Unlike conventional medicine, no two patients with the same disease label will necessarily receive the same medicine. This individualised approach is what makes homoeopathy so effective for chronic and treatment-resistant conditions.

  • Detailed 45–60 minute first consultation for thorough case analysis
  • Individualised constitutional prescription — not one-size-fits-all treatment
  • Regular follow-ups to track progress and adjust treatment
  • Patient education on diet, lifestyle and disease management
  • Transparent communication and compassionate, non-judgemental care
